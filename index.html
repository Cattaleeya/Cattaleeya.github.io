Project Idea - Multi Station Indoor Plant Irrigation System

Overview
Nowadays, many people would love to have plants inside their house or apartment but there is always a problem at the same time as taking a holiday, leaving your plants at home unattended and worrying about regardless of whether they will still be alive when you get home. In this situation, you need an automatic irrigation channel (like outdoor systems) to individualise the plants watering requirement. Nevertheless, if you have different size plants that require varying amounts of water, creating multiple channels or vales is the best solution that I can offer for this IT project.

Motivation
During taking a holiday and nobody at home to watering your favourite plants and make you worry about whether they will still alive. Unless you have a neighbour who you can trust enough to let into your house, consequently once you return home, you frequently see your plants die and/or dehydrated. At this point you even try to change the new soil, change location, feed them more plant food, try to keep them alive and look healthy again. However, most of the time it is disappointing attempted, and in the end, your lovely, beautiful plants are still gone.

Description
There are several types of automatic indoor plants watering system in the market, but then they are only a single station, meaning one water line feeds all plants at the same time at the same rate. The problem with this is if you have different sized plants that require varying amounts of water. For instance, you have a large fern in a 30 cm pot and a small begonia in a 12 cm pot then one water line could provide either the correct amount of water for the fern whilst at the same time overwatering and probably overflowing the begonia or the right amount of water for the begonia and under watering the fern. In this situation, you need multiple channels or valves (like outdoor systems) to individualise the plants watering requirements; therefore, creating a multi-station indoor plant irrigation system is a great features for your indoor plants' solution. Setting up an irrigation system effectively is important to maintain healthy plants. It is important that you have evaluated your level of water, schedule depending on the maturity of your plants, their growing cycle, and the time of years. You can set up how many times a day for watering your plants, how much water your plants will need in different plants sized and you can monitor it elsewhere in the world via your smartphone, not just monitoring but also you can control the timer and water level by using Raspberry Pi application (Hallek 2020).

In facts, in general all systems of automation have advantages and disadvantages that need to be considered when deciding which system will suit the irrigation layout for a particular property. No system will be the best system for all properties, so you need to write your code and let do not worry anything else except making sure you create it to serve your plants purposes.

Therefore, if you are planning a vacation, love travel or need to go out somewhere with friends, colleagues and family but often forget to feed your plant. This is exactly the type of system that will keep your plants beautiful, healthy, and watered. You don’t need to ask a friend or neighbour. Unless you trust them enough to let into your house or apartment then there are only a few options available to you. Therefore, the Multi watering plants system is your perfect solution as the automatic watering system will take care of your lovely plants for you while you are away.

Tools and Technologies
DIY Drippers: Drip Irrigation for Hydroponics (2015) shows how to make multiple station indoor plants irrigation system, at least you need to have a minimum component to build this multi-system. The following elements that you can be used for this project are as below sample:

  • Electronic breadboard
  • Raspberry Pi module to be determined
  • Python programming language
  • Several Solenoid valves and relays
  • Several moisture sensors
  • Several electrical wiring
  • Battery
  • Switches
  • LCDs display panel
  • Water tanks
  • Water tubing

Additionally, if you want to be able to control and monitor your plants while you are on vacation anywhere in the world, then you should be considering installing a smart phone application which will be considered at the latest date smart phone application, controlling and monitoring.

Skill Required
To build this specific project, you need to have skills and knowledges of how to use Python software for programming and coding as well as some electronic engineering knowledge for the Raspberry Pi.

Outcome
According to my research about the effectiveness of automated irrigation system is quite impressive, it’s reliable, efficient, and accurate. It always delivers the exact time and the amount of water when you need it as well as there is no chance of flooding your house while you are on holidays. Moreover, you do not need to ask for your friend or neighbour to take care of your lovely healthy indoor plants. Accordingly, the best part is that you don’t even need to be home when your plants need to be watered.
